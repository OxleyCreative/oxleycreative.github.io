Helping shoppers find the wheel that looks right

The Wheel Visualizer answers the question at the heart of wheel shopping: “How will it look on my car?” It combines vehicle and product photography with vehicle-color selection, filtering, and visual browsing so shoppers can quickly explore different styles. By bringing appearance into the shopping experience, the tool helps customers narrow their options and choose with greater confidence.
