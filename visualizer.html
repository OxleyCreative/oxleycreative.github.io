Finding the Wheel That Looks Right For You

Sometimes it’s about the numbers. Sometimes it’s just about the looks. We have found that when users are looking for wheels, there’s usually only one question on their minds. “How does it look on my car?” To answer that question we have a feature called the Wheel Visualizer. By harnessing our catalog of vehicle and product photography, it allows us to give users a pretty good idea of how a wheel will match their ride.

Keep On Scrollin' Scrollin' Scrollin'

The goal behind the wheel visualizer is to give users a way to shop more visually. Instead of being bogged down with all the product information that is on the product tiles, the user can quickly and easily scroll through the various wheel designs and compare them. Because users’ decisions on wheel selection are so visually based, we decided to position the visualizer before all the product tiles. Another important feature of the visualizer is giving users the ability to select the color of their vehicle, because it’s hard to judge aesthetics without having the right color.

Kickin' It Old School

We decided to keep the print feature because analytics showed that some customers do still use it. If they want to kick it old school and print off a .pdf to show their friends, that’s fine by us. At the end of the day the feature is all about getting people excited about how a product will look on their car, and helping them find the right one.
